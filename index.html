What is ATIEA?

Arizona Technology and Industrial Education Association

The ATIEA is the professional association for Career and Technical Education (CTE) teachers in the following program areas: communication technologies, transportation technologies, industrial technologies, manufacturing technologies, engineering, and human services (fire fighting and police science) at the pre-secondary, secondary and post-secondary levels. We are proudly affiliated with the Association for Career and Technical Education (ACTE and ACTE-AZ) and the International Technology and Engineering Education Association (ITEEA).

ATIEA

Constitution

Article I - Name

Sec. 1

The name of the Association shall be Arizona Technology and Industrial Education Association


Article II - Objectives

Sec. 1

Foster a spirit of mutual cooperation and unity among the members of the fields of Technology, Trade and Industry, and Technical Education.


Sec. 2

Promote understanding of several programs of Technology and Industrial Education on the part of school administrators, other educational personnel, and the general public.


Sec. 3

Promote links between academics and applied academic subjects.


Sec. 4

Enlist the assistance of associated organizations in the promotion of Technology and Industrial Education in Arizona.


Sec. 5

Encourage teachers of Technology and Industrial Education to participate in the development of solutions to problems associated with their field.


Sec. 6

Provide a means for exchanging ideas, learning strategies, and stimulate professional growth.


Sec. 7

Promote and assist teachers in the organization of affiliated student organizations related to Technology and Industrial Education.


Sec. 8

Promote Technology and Industrial Education by helping capable students become teachers in the field.
